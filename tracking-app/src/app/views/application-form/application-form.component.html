ACSIT Spring 2023 Delivery & Scheduling Restrictions - PT Faculty Form

We are in the process of delivery planning for the Spring 2023 term. We anticipate that 90% of our course will be in-person delivery at the Waterloo, Downtown Kitchener, or Guelph campuses. The following questions are intended to help us better understand our staffing needs and begin planning teaching assignments.


If you have any questions before completing and submitting this form please contact your Program Manager:

The deadline for submitting your availability restrictions for Spring 2023 term is Friday January 27 @ 4pm.


Please note the following:


1) Spring is our lightest load of courses, your assignments may be less than in Fall and Winter. If you do not receive a teaching assignment in the Spring you will still be considered for Fall 2023.


2) Spring 2023 term dates: Monday May 8th to Saturday August 19th (inclusive)


3) All faculty are required to be a resident of Ontario in order to be eligible to receive a contract of employment. Out of country access to Conestoga's systems is restricted. Any travel planned during the semester period should be disclosed in advance and will require pre-approval.


4) Until further notice, all employees must be compliant with our COVID Community Safety Policy in order to be eligible to receive a contract of employment. Proof of vaccination must be required.
